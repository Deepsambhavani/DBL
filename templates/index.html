🏸 Deep Badminton League

Dynamic Team Management System · DBL 2026

🎯
Scoring System
  • Matches are played best of 3 sets.
  • Each set is played to 21 points.
  • A player must win by 2 clear points.
  • If the score reaches 29-29, the next point wins (30 is the cap).
  • The side winning a rally adds a point to its score.
🏟️
Court & Service
  • Court size: 13.4m x 6.1m (doubles) / 5.18m wide (singles).
  • Net height: 1.55m at posts, 1.524m at centre.
  • Service must be hit below the server's waist.
  • The shuttle must land in the diagonally opposite service box.
  • Only one fault in service is allowed - second fault loses the rally.
🪶
Shuttle & Equipment
  • A shuttlecock has 16 feathers (or synthetic equivalent).
  • Shuttle weight: 4.74 - 5.50 grams.
  • Racket max length: 680mm; width: 230mm.
  • Players must wear non-marking shoes on court.
  • Shuttle speed varies with altitude and temperature.
⚡
During Play
  • A rally is won when the shuttle lands inside the opponent's court.
  • Hitting the shuttle twice (double hit) loses the rally.
  • Touching the net or posts with racket or body is a fault.
  • The shuttle must not be caught or slung with the racket.
  • Players switch ends after each set, and in the 3rd set at 11 points.
🔄
Lets & Faults
  • A let is called when play is interrupted unexpectedly.
  • The server must not delay service unreasonably.
  • Stepping on the boundary line during service is a fault.
  • Shuttle stuck in net on service = let; during play = fault.
  • A player must not obstruct or distract an opponent.
🏅
DBL Tournament Format
  • Up to 8 teams enter the bracket.
  • Teams are randomly seeded before the quarter-finals.
  • A coin toss decides who serves first in each match.
  • Winners advance; losers are eliminated (single elimination).
  • The last team standing is crowned DBL Champion 🏆.
